Canopy Guard - Free Website Visibility and Security Audit

What does Canopy Guard check?

Canopy Guard scans any website in 15 seconds across 47 signals covering four layers: SEO (14 signals including crawlability, meta descriptions, canonical URLs, internal linking, response time, and image alt text), AEO (10 signals including FAQ schema, Organization schema, JSON-LD validation, and Q&A content density), GEO (8 signals including chunking efficiency, citation precision, and llms.txt status), and Security (15 signals including TLS, HSTS, 6 security headers, cookie flags, AI crawl policy, and exposed endpoints).

What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization measures how well AI answer engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Overview, and Perplexity can extract and cite your content. Without proper schema markup and structured content, your site may be invisible to the AI tools that millions of people now use to find businesses and services.

What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization measures how well generative AI models can chunk, retrieve, and cite your pages in their responses. This includes checking whether your content breaks cleanly into retrieval chunks, whether your claims are specific and citable, and whether you have an llms.txt file guiding how AI crawlers reference your content.

What is Cross-Reference Intelligence?

Cross-Reference Intelligence maps visibility findings against security findings to surface gaps that only exist in the overlap. For example, a permissive robots.txt combined with no llms.txt file means AI crawlers can scrape your content without any citation guidance. Your expertise gets summarized in AI answers but your brand never gets linked back to. No SEO tool or security scanner flags this independently.

How accurate are the scores?

The scoring methodology is published openly at thecanopyguard.com. Every signal and every weight is documented so anyone can verify how their score was calculated. The tool was validated by scanning a production site that initially scored 11 out of 100, fixing every issue the tool flagged, and watching the score climb to 93 as each fix deployed.
